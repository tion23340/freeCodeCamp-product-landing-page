Features

Noice Cancelling AirPods noise cancellation is a technology that uses built-in microphones to detect and counteract external sounds, effectively reducing or eliminating background noise, allowing for a quieter and more immersive listening experience

Transparency ModeTransparency Mode allows you to hear your surroundings while still listening to audio through the AirPods Pro. This is useful when you want to remain aware of your environment, such as during conversations or when walking in a busy area.

EqualizerAirPods Pro include EQ (equalizer) settings that allow you to adjust the audio profile to suit your preferences. You can choose from several presets like "Bass Boost," "Treble Boost," and more, or manually customize the EQ settings.
